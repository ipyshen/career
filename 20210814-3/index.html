Audio

<audio>：add an audio
Simple version 
        <audio src="../asset/audio/Never-Gonna-Give-You-Up.mp3" controls>
Better version 
        <audio controls>
            <source src="../asset/audio/Never-Gonna-Give-You-Up.wav" type="audio/wav">
            <source src="../asset/audio/Never-Gonna-Give-You-Up.mp3" type="audio/mp3">
            Not found message. 
        </audio>
Attribute of <audio>
  • autoplay
  • controls
  • loop
  • muted
  • preload
